# Review request scripts — FPLUS

Use after successful job completion. Only ask real customers.

## WhatsApp (primary)

```
Hi [Name], thank you for choosing FPLUS Technical Services for your [AC repair / HVAC / plumbing / fit-out] in [area].

If you were happy with our work, a short Google review helps other Dubai customers find us. It takes about 1 minute:

[Insert your Google review link from GBP → Home → Ask for reviews → Copy link]

Thank you — FPLUS Team · +971 50 720 4645
```

## SMS / short version

```
Thanks for choosing FPLUS! Quick Google review? [review link] — helps our Dubai AC & HVAC team. Appreciate it!
```

[Google review link]

## When NOT to ask

- Job not fully completed or disputed
- Commercial client with NDAs (ask verbally first)
- Incentivized reviews (against Google policy)

## Getting your review link

1. Open [Google Business Profile](https://business.google.com)
2. **Home** → **Get more reviews** → copy short link
3. Save in `seo/review-link.txt` (create locally; do not commit if sensitive)
